Bienvenue!

Let us tell you about our trip!

We're Jess and Bill and this our Rick Steves tour scrapbook! In April 2022, we took the seven-day Rick Steves' Best of Paris tour. These pages and photos tell the story of our Parisian adventure, as well as a few side trips we took on our own. This was our first trip to continental Europe and we learned a lot on the tour! We included a ton of travel tips, so you can relax and have as much fun as we did. Browse through each individual day of the trip using the menu above. Below you can find a map of our trip, some more about who we are, and a few highlights.

“We learned a lot on the tour! We included a ton of tips, so you can relax and have as much fun as we did.”

We'd been planning our first trip to France in early 2020, but the COVID-19 pandemic had put those plans on hold. When Rick Steves announced that they were starting tours again for 2022, we were really excited, and decided it was time to make our travel dreams a reality. We chose Rick Steves because back in 2017, we went on the best trip we've ever taken, traveling around the UK on Rick Steves' Best of England & Wales tour. We had an amazing time, exploring cozy villages, and enjoying the beautiful city of Bath.

Enchanté!

"Nice to Meet You!"
Bonjour! I’m Jess, a novice traveller who loves art museums, fashion, gardening, dessert, and city life. I wanted to go to Paris because it’s home to some of the best examples of my favorite things. I love trying new things and I enjoyed all the history and knowledge I gained from our wonderful tour guide, Marie.

I spent some time studying French during the pandemic, and was looking forward to a chance to try it out in the real world.

Notre Hôtel

"Our Hotel"

We stayed at Hotel Londres Eiffel, a stylish boutique hotel in the 7th Arrondissement (district) of Paris, just blocks from the Eiffel Tower and Rue Cler, a beautiful shopping street full of tasty food and flowers. The staff were super helpful: Arnaud at the front desk recommended restaurants and helped us book taxis. The tour group hung out in the breakfast room every morning and even enjoyed drinks together in the lobby lounge one evening. We also had the pleasure of a rare visit from Samba, the hotel owner’s Golden Retriever (seen above!).
